List of Implementations

LWQ has been implemented across a variety of software and hardware platforms to demonstrate its flexibility and efficiency in different environments.

Software - Reference C

Platform independent. Focuses on clarity, constant-time execution, and ease of auditing. Compliant with C99 standards.

Software - AVX2 Optimized

Targets modern Intel and AMD processors. Utilizes 256-bit SIMD instructions to significantly accelerate polynomial multiplication and sampling.

Embedded - ARM Cortex-M4

Highly optimized assembly implementation for constrained IoT environments. Achieves minimal memory footprint while maintaining competitive cycle counts.

Hardware - FPGA (Verilog)

A fast, constant-time hardware accelerator designed for Xilinx Artix-7 FPGAs. Demonstrates the hardware efficiency of LWQ's power-of-two moduli design.
